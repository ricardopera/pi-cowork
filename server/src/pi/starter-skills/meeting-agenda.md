# Build an Agenda

When asked to build a meeting agenda:

1. State the meeting goal and desired outcome in one line.
2. List timed agenda items with owners; keep it under the meeting length.
3. Mark decision items vs. discussion vs. updates.
4. Add pre-reads and a "prep" note for attendees.
5. Export as create_markdown or create_docx.
