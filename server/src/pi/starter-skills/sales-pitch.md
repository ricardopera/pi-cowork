# Build a Sales Pitch

When asked to craft a pitch:

1. Lead with the customer's problem in their words.
2. State the solution and the 2-3 differentiators that matter to them.
3. Back claims with proof (data, case study, demo).
4. End with a concrete, low-friction ask.
5. Tailor to the persona; keep under 3 minutes spoken.
