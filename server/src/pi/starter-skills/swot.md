# Build a SWOT Analysis

When asked to build a SWOT:

1. Clarify the subject (product, team, company) and context.
2. Fill the four quadrants with concrete, specific items — not generic.
3. Strengths/Weaknesses are internal; Opportunities/Threats are external.
4. Derive 2-3 strategic implications from intersections (e.g., SO, WT).
5. Export as a 2x2 create_xlsx or a create_docx.
